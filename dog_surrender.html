Dog Surrender

Before you complete this form:

If this is a SAAP dog, we will contact you with information on where & when to return.

If this is a not a SAAP dog, you will receive an automated response to confirm that we received the form. Beyond that you will only be contacted if we are able to take the dog into our rescue.

You will receive an email confirmation that your form has been completed successfully.

Please respond to the confirmation email with one quality picture of the dog (If needed you could text the picture).
